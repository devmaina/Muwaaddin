Muwaaddin Platform waa mareeg warbaahineed, waxa ay diiradda saaraysaa arrimaha siyaasadeed, bulsheed iyo dhaqan ee Soomaaliya ka taagan. Waxa lagu soo bandhigi doonaa qoraallo tayo leh oo wacyiga bulshada wax ku biiriya. Waxay noqon doontaa mareeg ummadda Soomaaliyeed u siman tahay, oo baahisa aragtiyo madaxbannaan, falanqaynno siyaasadeed, bulsheed iyo dhaqan-dhaqaale, iyada oo kabaysa saxaafadda qoraalka ee Soomaalida. Muwaddin ma noqon doonto mareeg cid gaar ah u xaglinaysa.


Muwaaddin Platform is a media website dedicated to addressing Somalia’s political, social, and cultural issues. It will feature high-quality articles that raise public awareness and foster critical dialogue. The platform aims to be inclusive, providing space for all Somalis by publishing independent perspectives alongside political, social, and socio-economic analyses. Muwaaddin seeks to complement Somali written journalism while maintaining neutrality, avoiding bias toward any group or interest.
